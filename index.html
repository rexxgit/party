About Our Vision

Meet Dawit

Born from a unique cultural fusion, HagerBeat Entertainment represents the harmonious blend of Swiss precision and Ethiopian soul. Founded by Dawit, our vision is to create experiences that transcend borders and unite hearts through the universal language of music, film, and unforgettable events.

We believe that the most beautiful creations emerge when different cultures meet, creating something entirely new while honoring the richness of each tradition. Our work embodies this philosophy, bringing together the meticulous craftsmanship of Swiss culture with the vibrant, soulful energy of Ethiopian heritage.
